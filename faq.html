Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM onwards, and check-out is by 11:00 AM.

2. Does the hotel offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi in all rooms and common areas of the hotel.

3. Are pets allowed at Hotel Athira?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at Hotel Athira. However, service animals are welcome.

4. Can I request an early check-in or late check-out?

Yes, you can request an early check-in or late check-out, subject to availability. Please contact the front desk ahead of time to make arrangements.

5. Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we have free parking available for guests at the hotel, based on availability.

6. Does the hotel offer room service?

Yes, room service is available from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day. You can order food and beverages to your room by contacting the front desk.

7. Are there any nearby attractions to visit?

Yes, Hotel Athira is located near several popular attractions, including parks, shopping malls, and historical landmarks. Please check with the front desk for recommendations and directions.

8. How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation directly on our website or by calling our reservations team. Alternatively, you can also book through third-party websites like Booking.com or Expedia.
